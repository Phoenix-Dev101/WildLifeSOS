About the Wildlife Protection Amendment Bill 2022

The Wildlife Protection Amendment Bill 2022 is a landmark legislation aimed at strengthening wildlife conservation efforts and addressing the challenges faced by endangered species. It enforces stricter regulations to combat poaching, habitat destruction, and illegal wildlife trade. Through this bill, we can ensure the long-term survival and well-being of our diverse wildlife.
